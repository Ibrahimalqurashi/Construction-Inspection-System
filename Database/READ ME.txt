this is the database for the project

import the "testdb.sql" to phpMyAdmin to test the project.

tools:
-xampp (should include MariaDB)

steps:
1- install xamp.
2- go to "C:\xampp"
3- run "xampp-control.exe"
4- in the "Actions" coullumn click "Start" for "Apache" and "MySQL".
5- open up the browser and go to "http://localhost/phpmyadmin".
6- on the right click on "New" then click on the sixth tab called "Import".
7- click on "Choose File" and select "testdb.sql" then click on go.
8- cheack that the database has been imported, it should appear on the right under "New".
